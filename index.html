Holistic working at La Serre

La Serre is a lifestyle laboratory where naturopathy concepts are woven together with a collaborative space for professionals. Well-being is a focus here. Because we believe that when you work on yourself, your business and projects flourish. That’s what we consider the ultimate work-life balance.

Entrepreneurs + therapists + medical pros

Playing off each others’ strengths, entrepreneurs, therapists and medtech innovators (not to mention designers, teachers and developers) bring La Serre to life. Here, coworking sprouts in private life as our members balance inner work with collaboration and teamwork with others.

Healthy lifestyle at a discount

Naturopathy specialists and therapists can run their own practices out of La Serre. Members can benefit from their services, too, because they are included as part of the membership fee. The path to success is paved with herbal remedies.

Food for thought

At our on-site cafe, you can enjoy tea, coffee or juice, fresh snacks and meals, and space to store your own home-cooked meals. What’s most important is that you have the nourishment you need to keep thriving.
